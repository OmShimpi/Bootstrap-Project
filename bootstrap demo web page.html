Founder Of Company :

ODesign is the ultimate high-end interior design company, run by renowned interior designer Rohit Chaitanya. The designer's own luxury homes and lavish lifestyle are the perfect advertisement for the opulent and stylish interior designs the company has become famous for. Working predominantly in the style of “contemporary classic”, the team describes its interior design work as “livable art”, a combination of client preferences and the firm's own artistic vision, led by Rohit, which come together to create beautiful, bespoke, and ornate spaces that exceed the expectations of clients while wowing visitors and guests.
